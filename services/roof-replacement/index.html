A Complete Roofing System, Installed Right

A new roof is one of the largest investments you'll make in your home, and in Florida it's also one of the most important. Your roof is your first line of defense against hurricane-force wind, driving summer rain, and relentless UV exposure. At Redefine Roofing, we replace roofs as a complete system — decking inspection, underlayment, flashing, ventilation, and shingles that are all engineered to work together — so nothing about your new roof is a weak link.

As an Atlas Manufacturer Certified installer, we install the Atlas Signature Select Roofing System, with every component rated to meet and exceed local building codes. Because we don't mix and match materials from different manufacturers, your full manufacturer warranty stays intact — a detail many homeowners only discover matters when it's too late.

Signs It's Time to Replace Your Roof

  • Your shingle roof is 15–20+ years old — Florida sun and humidity age roofs faster than national averages
  • Shingles are curling, cracking, or losing granules (check your gutters for granule buildup)
  • Repeated leaks or repairs in different spots — patching has stopped making financial sense
  • Storm damage across large sections of the roof, not just one area
  • Your insurance carrier is asking about roof age at renewal time

Not sure whether you need a repair or a full replacement? We'll tell you honestly. Our estimates are free and no-pressure — if a roof repair will safely extend your roof's life, that's what we'll recommend.

What to Expect: Our Replacement Process

  1. Free inspection & detailed written quote. We document the condition of your roof, walk you through your options, and give you a line-item quote with no hidden fees.
  2. Permitting handled for you. Every Tampa Bay county requires a permit for roof replacement. As a licensed Florida contractor (CCC 1331457), we pull and manage the permit and schedule all required inspections.
  3. Tear-off and deck inspection. We remove the old roof completely and inspect the decking, replacing any rotted or damaged wood before anything new goes on.
  4. System installation. Underlayment, drip edge, flashing, ventilation, and shingles installed to manufacturer spec by background-checked crews.
  5. Cleanup and final walkthrough. Full site cleanup with a magnetic nail sweep of your yard and driveway, then a walkthrough with you to make sure every detail meets our standard — and yours.

Most residential replacements are completed in one to three days, and we keep you informed at every step.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a roof replacement cost in Tampa Bay?

Cost depends on your roof's size, pitch, material choice, and decking condition, which is why we provide free, detailed written quotes rather than ballpark figures. Every quote is line-itemed with no hidden fees, and we never use high-pressure sales tactics — we'd rather educate you than sell you.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in the Tampa Bay area are completed in one to three days once materials arrive, weather permitting. Complex roofs or homes needing significant decking repair can take longer — we'll give you a realistic timeline in your written quote.

Do I need a permit to replace my roof in Florida?

Yes. Every county we serve — Hillsborough, Pinellas, Pasco, Manatee, Sarasota, and Hernando — requires a roofing permit for replacement. As your licensed contractor, Redefine Roofing pulls the permit and manages all required inspections, so you don't have to handle any of the paperwork.

Will a new roof lower my homeowners insurance?

It often helps. Many Florida carriers offer wind mitigation credits for roofs installed to current code with features like sealed roof decks and proper nailing patterns. After your replacement, a wind mitigation inspection can document these features for your insurer. A newer roof can also make your home easier to insure at renewal time.

Can I stay home during the replacement?

Absolutely. Most homeowners do. Expect noise during tear-off and installation, and we'll ask you to move vehicles out of the driveway. We protect landscaping, keep the site organized, and finish with a full cleanup including a magnetic nail sweep.
