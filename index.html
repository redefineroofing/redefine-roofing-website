Our Story

About ReDEFINE Roofing

Founded on the belief that homeowners deserve better — better communication, better materials, and better craftsmanship — Redefine Roofing has been protecting homes across the region for over 15 years.

What started as a small family operation has grown into a full-service residential roofing company with a team of certified installers, dedicated project managers, and customer service specialists who treat every home like their own.

We are proud members of the National Roofing Contractors Association (NRCA) and hold certifications from leading manufacturers including Atlas and CertainTeed — giving our customers access to the best warranty programs in the industry.
