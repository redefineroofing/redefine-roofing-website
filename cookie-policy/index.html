Cookie Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Redefine Roofing uses cookies and similar technologies on redefineroofing.com (the "Site"). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, measure how the site is used, and support advertising. "Pixels" and similar technologies serve related purposes and are covered by this policy as well.

Cookies We Use

Essential / Functional

Our Site is a static website hosted on Netlify and works without requiring login or account cookies. Hosting and form-submission infrastructure may use limited technical cookies or identifiers strictly to deliver the Site securely and process form submissions.

Analytics — Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the Site — pages viewed, time on site, and how visitors found us. Google Analytics sets cookies such as _ga and _ga_*, which contain a randomly generated identifier (not your name). These typically persist for up to 2 years. Learn more in Google's cookie documentation.

Advertising — Meta (Facebook) Pixel

We use the Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising on Facebook and Instagram and to understand actions visitors take on the Site (such as viewing pages or submitting an estimate request). The Meta Pixel may set cookies such as _fbp (typically persisting about 90 days) and, where applicable, read the fr cookie set by Facebook. Learn more in Meta's Cookies Policy.

Third-Party Tools You Choose to Use

If you open the "Free Instant Estimate" tool, it loads content from Roofr, which may set its own cookies to operate the estimator. The estimator loads only when you open it. Roofr's practices are governed by Roofr's own privacy and cookie policies.

How to Control Cookies

  • Browser settings: All major browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking all cookies may affect how parts of the Site work. See your browser's help pages for instructions.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Meta ad controls: Manage how Meta uses your data for advertising in your Facebook Ad Preferences, and learn about industry opt-outs at aboutads.info.
  • Mobile settings: iOS and Android offer device-level controls that limit ad tracking across apps and sites.

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" signal. Like most websites, the Site does not currently respond to DNT signals, because no common industry standard for them has been adopted. The opt-out tools above remain available regardless of DNT settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our Site or the technologies we use change. The Effective Date above shows the latest revision, and the current version will always be posted here.
