Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

Redefine Roofing ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website, redefineroofing.com (the "Site"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Who We Are

Redefine Roofing is a residential roofing contractor licensed in the State of Florida (License CCC 1331457), located at 801 West Bay Dr. Suite #113, Largo, FL 33770. You can reach us at info@redefineroofing.com or (813) 590-9050.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit our estimate request or contact form, we collect the information you enter: your name, email address, phone number, property address (optional), the service you're interested in, and any message you include. If you call, text, or email us, we collect the contact information and details you share in that communication.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, certain information is collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies, including your IP address, browser and device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and how you arrived at the Site. See our Cookie Policy for details on the specific technologies used.

Third-Party Tools

If you use the "Free Instant Estimate" tool on our Site, that tool is provided by Roofr, a third-party service. Information you enter there (such as your property address) is collected and processed by Roofr under its own privacy policy, and shared with us to prepare your estimate.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your estimate requests, questions, and service inquiries
  • To schedule, perform, and follow up on roofing services
  • To communicate with you about your project, including by phone, text message, and email where you have agreed to be contacted
  • To understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it
  • To measure and improve our advertising, including on platforms like Facebook and Instagram
  • To comply with legal obligations, including licensing, permitting, and warranty records

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the Site and our business — including Netlify (website hosting and form processing), Google (analytics), Meta (advertising measurement), and Roofr (instant estimates) — each of which processes data under its own privacy terms
  • Professional and legal parties where reasonably necessary, such as permitting authorities, insurance carriers you ask us to work with on a claim, or as required by law

Analytics and Advertising

We use Google Analytics to understand Site traffic and Meta (Facebook) Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. These tools use cookies and similar identifiers, and may associate your visit with other data those companies hold. You can opt out of Google Analytics with the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, and manage how Meta uses your data for ads in your Facebook Ad Preferences. Our Cookie Policy lists the specific cookies used.

Calls and Text Messages

Our contact form asks for your consent to be contacted about your inquiry. By providing your phone number and checking that box, you agree that we may call or text you regarding your request. Message and data rates may apply to texts. You can withdraw this consent at any time by replying STOP to a text, or by telling us by phone or email, and we will stop contacting you except as needed to complete work you've already engaged us for.

Data Retention

We keep inquiry and project records for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above — including honoring warranties, maintaining required contractor records, and defending legal claims — and then delete or anonymize them.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, including hosting the Site over HTTPS. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it (subject to records we're required to keep) by emailing info@redefineroofing.com
  • Marketing opt-out: You may opt out of marketing communications at any time; we will still send transactional messages related to work in progress
  • Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings and the opt-out tools described above and in our Cookie Policy

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under applicable law. We honor valid requests as required by the laws that apply to us.

Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for homeowners and other adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Third-Party Links

The Site links to third-party sites and services (such as our social media pages and Google Maps). Those sites operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" above shows when it was last revised, and the current version will always be posted on this page.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your information? Contact us at info@redefineroofing.com, (813) 590-9050, or Redefine Roofing, 801 West Bay Dr. Suite #113, Largo, FL 33770.
