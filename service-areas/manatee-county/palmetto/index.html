Serving Palmetto, Florida

Palmetto Roof Repair & Replacement — North of the River

On the north bank of the Manatee River, Palmetto keeps its old-Florida character — and its older roofs deserve repair-first thinking, not reflexive tear-off quotes.

(813) 590-9050 Get a Free Estimate

Old Florida Deserves a Lighter Touch

Palmetto's historic district and the streets off Riverside Drive hold some of Manatee County's most characterful housing — riverfront homes and agricultural-era buildings that have watched a century of Gulf weather go by. Old roofs aren't automatically dead roofs. Our first instinct in Palmetto is preservation: trace the leak, repair the failure properly, replace the decking that needs it, and leave the rest of a sound roof alone. When repair genuinely serves the home better than replacement, that's the quote you get.

And When It Is Time, We Say So Plainly

Some Palmetto roofs are past honest repair — decades of patches, widespread granule loss, decking going soft. Our free inspection settles the question with photos and a written remaining-life assessment, and if replacement is the truthful answer, our complete-system installations respect the character question too: profiles and colors that fit a historic street, built to modern wind code underneath. City of Palmetto permits handled by us.

North-Bank Exposure

Palmetto faces open approach for Gulf systems moving up the coast, and the river adds its own humidity load. That combination shows up as corroded flashing on older homes and wind-worked shingles after every serious season. Our storm damage team runs the same playbook here as everywhere: 24/7 response, same-day tarping when possible, full documentation, direct carrier coordination — with special care on older homes where storm damage and age-related wear need to be clearly distinguished for a claim to succeed.

Growing North County, Covered

North of town, new construction is filling in fast toward Ellenton and the US-301 corridor. Newer Palmetto homes get the services that fit them — post-storm inspections, ventilation checks, and baseline documentation — with the same free estimates, background-checked crews, and lifetime workmanship warranty as everything we touch.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you repair my older Palmetto roof instead of replacing it?

If the roof has sound life left — often yes, and it's our preferred first answer. Localized leaks, flashing failures, and storm-damaged sections on otherwise healthy roofs are repairable, and we'll show you photos of exactly why we're recommending repair or why we're not.

How do you handle historic character on Palmetto's riverfront homes?

Material and color choices that fit the home's era and the street, detail work at eaves and gables done by hand rather than production pace, and honest handling of the old decking underneath. Character homes are a craftsmanship assignment and we staff them that way.

Does Palmetto permit its own roofing work?

Yes — addresses inside city limits permit through the City of Palmetto, with Manatee County handling the surrounding areas. Both are routine for us and included in the job.

Do you cover the new neighborhoods north of Palmetto?

Yes — the growth corridor toward Ellenton and US-301 is fully inside our service area, along with everything between the river and the county line.
